The Problem

Why Bird Proofing Matters

Birds nesting under solar panels cause serious damage and reduce efficiency

Reduced Solar Efficiency

Nests and droppings block sunlight, reducing your panels' energy output by up to 25%.

Fire Hazards

Dry nesting materials near electrical wiring create serious fire risks for your home.

Roof Damage

Acidic bird droppings corrode roofing materials, leading to leaks and costly repairs.

Health Concerns

Bird droppings carry diseases and parasites that can affect your family's health.

What We Do

Complete Solar Panel Protection

We provide full service solar panel bird proofing and maintenance. Our team handles everything from initial cleaning to final protection, ensuring your panels are clean, safe, and protected from future bird activity.

1

Solar Panel Surface Cleaning

We thoroughly clean the top surface of your solar panels, removing all bird droppings, debris, and buildup that reduces efficiency. Using safe, eco friendly methods that won't damage your panels or void warranties.

2

Under-Panel Cleaning & Sanitization

Complete removal of nests, droppings, and debris from underneath your solar panels. We sanitize the area to eliminate parasites, bacteria, and odors, protecting both your roof and your family's health.

3

Bird Proofing Mesh Installation

Professional installation of durable, warranty-compliant wire mesh around the perimeter of your solar panels. This prevents birds from accessing the space underneath while maintaining proper airflow and panel function.

4

Roof Spike Installation

Strategic placement of humane bird deterrent spikes on roof peaks, edges, and other landing areas. These prevent birds from roosting near your panels without causing harm, providing long term protection.

5

Gutter Cleaning

As part of our comprehensive service, we clean your gutters to remove bird related debris, leaves, and buildup. This ensures proper drainage and prevents water damage to your home's foundation and roof.

Complete Service, One Visit

We handle everything in a single appointment. From initial assessment to final cleanup, our experienced team provides comprehensive protection for your solar investment with minimal disruption to your day.
